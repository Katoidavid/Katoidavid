Welcome to PHOEMAR ORGANIC FARM

Your trusted provider of fresh produce and poultry in Kathiani, Kitui County.

About Us

Phoemar Farm is a dedicated agricultural enterprise situated at Kathiani, Kitui County. We specialize in the cultivation of onions, tomatoes, and sukuma wiki, along with poultry and bee farming.

Our Products

Onions

High-quality onions grown organically to ensure the best flavor and nutrition.

Tomatoes

Fresh, juicy tomatoes perfect for your kitchen needs.

Sukuma Wiki

Nutritious sukuma wiki, a staple in many Kenyan dishes.

Chicken

Healthy and free-range chickens raised with care.

Honey

Pure and natural honey from our well-maintained beehives.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide high-quality, fresh, and organic produce to our community while promoting sustainable farming practices.

Vision

To be the leading provider of fresh farm produce in Kitui County, recognized for our quality and commitment to sustainability.
